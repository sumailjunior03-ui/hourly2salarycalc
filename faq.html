Frequently Asked Questions

How is annual salary calculated?

Annual salary = hourly wage × hours per week × weeks per year. The default is 40 hours × 52 weeks = 2,080 total hours per year.

How is monthly salary calculated?

Monthly = annual salary ÷ 12.

How is bi-weekly pay calculated?

Bi-weekly = annual salary ÷ 26. There are 26 bi-weekly pay periods in a year.

Does this include taxes?

No. This tool calculates gross (pre-tax) salary equivalents only. Your take-home pay will be lower depending on your tax bracket, location, and deductions.

What if I work part-time?

Just change the hours per week field. For example, if you work 20 hours per week enter 20 and the tool will calculate accordingly.

What if I take unpaid weeks off?

Change the weeks per year field. If you take 2 weeks unpaid leave, enter 50 weeks instead of 52.

Does this tool store my data?

No. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
